# Recitation 11: Office Hours for P5 Project and Task Selection

## Overview

In this recitation, you will meet with your reci TAs and discuss potential open source projects for P5. Come prepared with 2-3 possible project ideas (and potentially task options for those projects) on Monday. Your TAs will give you feedback. 

## Submission

Before leaving recitation, **each student** should submit individually to [Gradescope](https://www.gradescope.com/courses/1213157/assignments/7930030):

1. Links to the open source projects you discussed with your TAs

2. **A brief summary** of the feedback your TAs gave your team
